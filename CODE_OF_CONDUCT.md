# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community will treat each other with respect and courtesy.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful**: Treat all community members with kindness and respect
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and support to others
- **Be professional**: Keep communications professional and appropriate
- **Report issues privately**: If you see a violation, please report it privately

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind
- Hate speech or offensive language
- Threats or violence
- Spam or inappropriate content
- Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent bans from the community
- Reporting to appropriate authorities if necessary

## Questions?

If you have questions about this code of conduct, please reach out to the project maintainers.
